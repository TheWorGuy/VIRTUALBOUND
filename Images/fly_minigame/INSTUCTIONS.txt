Aight so here's how the fly minigame will work.

/Function/

Once you enter this page, there will no longer be a "Next" button to press. You must swat the fly in order to proceed.
The way the game works is that for the first 15 seconds of you opening the page, the fly will be unswattable. Basically right as you hover your mouse over it, it'll fly away from the cursor at high speeds. If the player fails to hit the fly, it'll move them to a "Missed" page where there's a try again button. By this time hopefully the player will get tired of trying. After the 15 second timer is up, the fly will slowly move to the bottom left corner of the screen and start sleeping. Then and only then can the player hit it and move on to the next page.

/Sprites/

The "fly_game_backgound" image is the background image... obviously
The "fly_game_fly_1" and "fly_game_fly_2" are the sprites used when the fly is flying about, rapidly switching between both sprites.
The "fly_game_fly_sleep" is for when the fly goes to sleep... OBVIOUSLY
And finally the "fly_game_swat" is the image that hovers over your cursor. Make sure to rotate it vertically.
